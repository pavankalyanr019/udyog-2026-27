About Rotaract Club of Bangalore Udyog

Our Charter

Chartered July 18, 2023 (ID: 8825840), the Rotaract Club of Bangalore Udyog is a youth-led engine under Rotary International. Driven by Enthustic energy, we fuse leadership and professional growth with impactful community service. We innovate sustainable solutions through fellowship and industrious effort. Join us in building a bold legacy together.
